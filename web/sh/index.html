The KLM‑76 sample & hold of the PS‑3100: JFET switch, 0.1 µF hold capacitor, and a clock fitted to the panel's “~1.2 Hz at center” annotation - same Faust source as the plugin, compiled to WebAssembly. The scope draws the held voltage; the lamp is the S/H IND flash. Patched here the classic way: stepped random pitch. DROOP exaggerates the CA3140 bias-current sag between samples.
