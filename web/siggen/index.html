The KLM‑64E signal generator of the PS‑3100 - one of the 12 per-note cards that make it fully polyphonic. A relaxation VCO tuned by the cap chart, binary dividers, and the 8-level staircase waveshaper: sweep WAVE FORM from the saw family into the quantized-duty pulse family. The keybed picks (note, octave row) exactly as the key matrix does; gating is a bench convenience - the real card free-runs into the GEG/VCA.
