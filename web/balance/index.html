The KLM‑62D balance mixer and AM cell of the PS‑3100: the upper/lower keyboard channels meet a JFET ring-style modulator. In TWO‑TONE the board's internal carrier meets AM MOD IN - watch the sidebands appear on the scope as you raise AM INTENSITY and move MOD FREQ. In the keyboard modes the same cell is your channel mixer.
