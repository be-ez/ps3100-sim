The KLM‑76 Voltage Processors of the PS‑3100: two bipolar attenuverters for the patch field - CW non-inverting, CCW inverting, center null. The model solves the exact pot network on the shared −5 V bus, so the bus sag, the imperfect null, and the faint antiphase crosstalk between channels are all SPICE-exact. A slow sine feeds VP 1 and a DC level feeds VP 2; the scope draws the selected output.
