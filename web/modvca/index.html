The KLM‑63 MOD‑VCA and MG2 of the PS‑3100: an F201 vactrol gain cell (−42 dB dark to +2.8 dB lit) and the board's own triangle-core LFO. The scope draws MG2's asymmetric triangle straight from the model; flip MG2 → CONT to patch it into the VCA the way the panel pin-jacks do - vactrol lag and all. The lamp is the F201 LED.
