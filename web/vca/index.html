The KLM‑76 dual VCA of the PS‑3100: two P873 vactrol gain cells in series, here gated by the keybed the way GEG OUT drives VCA 1 CONT in the real instrument - the soft attack and slow release you hear is the vactrol, not an envelope generator. MG on VCA 2 gives tremolo; the scope draws the output envelope, and the lamps are the two vactrol LEDs.
