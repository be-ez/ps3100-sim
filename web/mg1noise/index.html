The KLM‑63 MG1 and noise generator of the PS‑3100: an exponential-law triangle-core LFO (≈0.59 V/oct on FREQ CONT I) with four output pins, and the 2SC644 avalanche noise source with its white and pink build-outs - the pink ladder is the exact factored s-domain transfer of the IC31b stage. LFO pins draw on the scope; noise pins are audible and spectrum-scoped.
