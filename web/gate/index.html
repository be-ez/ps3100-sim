One of the twelve KLM‑69E gate channels of the PS‑3100: a CD4007 pass-gate VCA with its RC envelope, then the KORG35 Sallen‑Key cell - reverse-saturated junctions whose distortion is part of the sound. Keys gate the channel; DRIVE pushes the mV-scale core into its H2-dominant saturation, CUTOFF is the FC bus voltage.
