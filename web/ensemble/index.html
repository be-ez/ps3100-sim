The KLM‑76 Ensemble of the PS‑3100: two MN3004 bucket-brigade delay lines, each clock-modulated by its own CD4069 phase-shift LFO (3.06 and 2.54 Hz, measured in SPICE), summed against the dry signal. Hold a chord - the beating between the two detuned channels is the ensemble. The lamps breathe with each channel's LFO.
